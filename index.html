About Chelsealicious


Chelsealicious is a combination of Chelsea’s favourite dishes all incorporated in one Restaurant in SW3. We are currently assembling a team of young workers for our Business in Chelsea. We offer refreshing smoothies, Chelsea’s favourite nibbles as well as a wide range of different cuisine favourites from all around the world.

We offer competitive prices for the area as well as providing a relaxed atmosphere and delicious food to everyone who wishes to enjoy the Chelsealicious experience.
